The History of LilyCakes Bakery How LilyCakes started

Learn more about Lily and her story!

How did Lily get into baking?

Lily grew up in a house where she was surrounded by sweets. Her mom baked bread for their local homeless shelter, and made cookie boxes for their neighbors. Naturally, Lily developed an interest in baking, after constantly assisting her mom in the kitchen.

Lily's mom is currently involved in the behind the scenes of the bakery. She brainstorms new treats to bake, and helps experiment with them to make them delicious for the customers.

What were the first items that Lily started to bake?

LilyCakes started her baking career with:

  1. Banana Bread
  2. Sugar Cookies
  3. Peanut Butter Cookies
